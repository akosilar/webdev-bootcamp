Location

I am a link about CCHS: Central Catholic High School Basketball Gym, 4699 Winthrop St., Pittsburgh PA 15213

Fees

  • PTTC membership: Adults $150 per year (Students $75), entitles players to free leagues, tourney, open play. Includes FREE membership to South Park TTC
  • Pay as you play:
    • ADULTS – $5 League (+ tourney), $5 Open play, $5 Junior League, $10/hour clinic.
    • STUDENTS – $2 League (+ tourney), $2 Open play, $2 Junior League, $5/hour clinic.
      • NOTE: $5 Sunday fee covers League + Tourney, Open Play and Novice Program.
